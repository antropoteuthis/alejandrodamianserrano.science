Media Presence

CreatureCast

I have worked with animator Riley Thompson to produce these animated outreach videos explaining the natural history of siphonophore tentilla, their development, and prey capture behaviors.

JEB Early Career Researcher Spotlight Interview

ECR Spotlight features interviews with early-career authors from the Journal of Experimental Biology, highlighting the diversity of researchers and animals in experimental biology. I conducted research on “Colonial architecture modulates the speed and efficiency of multi-jet swimming in salp colonies” as a postdoc in Kelly R. Sutherland's lab at the University of Oregon.
